## About Simplr

Simplr is a minimalist productivity suite built on the philosophy that less is more. In a world of cluttered task managers and overwhelming productivity apps, Simplr provides only the essential tools you need to stay organized and focused.

### Core Features

- **Clean Task Management**: Simple, distraction-free todo lists without unnecessary features
- **Note Taking**: Quick, lightweight notes for capturing ideas and information
- **Time Tracking**: Basic time tracking to understand how you spend your day
- **Focus Mode**: Minimize distractions and concentrate on what matters
- **Calendar Integration**: Simple calendar view for planning your day
- **Minimal Design**: Beautiful, uncluttered interface that stays out of your way

### Philosophy

Simplr embraces minimalism in productivity by:

- **Removing Complexity**: No overwhelming features or complicated setups
- **Reducing Friction**: Get started immediately without lengthy onboarding
- **Staying Focused**: Tools that help you do your work, not manage your tools
- **Respecting Time**: Fast, lightweight, and efficient
- **Promoting Clarity**: Clear visual hierarchy and intuitive interactions

### Perfect For

- **Minimalists**: Those who prefer simple, elegant solutions
- **Focus Seekers**: People overwhelmed by feature-heavy productivity apps
- **Quick Starters**: Users who want to be productive immediately
- **Clean Workspace Lovers**: Anyone who appreciates aesthetic simplicity

### Benefits

- **Reduced Cognitive Load**: Fewer decisions, more action
- **Faster Workflow**: Minimal learning curve means immediate productivity
- **Less Stress**: Simple tools for a simpler, more organized life
- **Better Focus**: Remove distractions and concentrate on your goals

### Design Principles

- **Clarity Over Features**: Every element serves a purpose
- **Speed Over Complexity**: Fast and responsive, always
- **Beauty in Simplicity**: Elegant design that's pleasing to use
- **Privacy Focused**: Your data stays yours

Experience productivity the way it should be—simple, effective, and stress-free.
