## About ZimCharades

ZimCharades is an entertaining mobile game that brings the classic game of charades into the digital age with a distinctly Zimbabwean flavor. Perfect for parties, family gatherings, and social events, this app celebrates Zimbabwean culture through fun and interactive gameplay.

### How to Play

1. **Gather Your Friends**: The more the merrier! Works best with groups of 3 or more
2. **Choose a Category**: Select from various Zimbabwean-themed categories
3. **Act It Out**: Hold the phone to your forehead and act out the word while others guess
4. **Tilt to Score**: Tilt down when guessed correctly, tilt up to skip
5. **Beat the Clock**: Score as many points as possible before time runs out

### Game Categories

- **Zimbabwean Celebrities**: Local musicians, actors, politicians, and public figures
- **Landmarks**: Famous places and monuments across Zimbabwe
- **Local Slang**: Popular Zimbabwean expressions and colloquialisms
- **Traditional Culture**: Customs, traditions, and cultural practices
- **Food & Cuisine**: Zimbabwean dishes and local delicacies
- **Sports**: Local athletes and sports moments
- **History**: Important historical events and figures

### Key Features

- **Offline Play**: No internet connection required
- **Multiple Difficulty Levels**: Easy, medium, and hard challenges
- **Custom Decks**: Create your own card decks with personalized words
- **Score Tracking**: Keep track of high scores and compete with friends
- **Family-Friendly**: Content appropriate for all ages
- **Regular Updates**: New words and categories added frequently
- **Deck Sharing**: Share custom decks with other players
