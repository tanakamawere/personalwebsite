## About Marketplace Application

Marketplace Application is a comprehensive e-commerce platform that connects buyers and sellers in a user-friendly online marketplace. Built with modern web technologies and hosted on Azure Static Web Apps, this application provides a robust foundation for online commerce.

### Key Features

- **Product Listings**: Create detailed product listings with multiple images, descriptions, and pricing information
- **Category Organization**: Browse products by categories for easy navigation and discovery
- **Shopping Cart**: Add multiple items to your cart and checkout seamlessly
- **User Accounts**: Secure registration and login system for both buyers and sellers
- **Seller Dashboard**: Manage your products, track sales, and update inventory
- **Search Functionality**: Powerful search engine to help buyers find exactly what they're looking for
- **Product Reviews**: Rate and review products to help other buyers make informed decisions
- **Secure Checkout**: Safe and secure payment processing for peace of mind

### For Sellers

The platform empowers sellers to:
- Set up their online store quickly and easily
- Upload product images and create compelling descriptions
- Manage inventory and track orders
- Communicate with customers
- Grow their business with built-in marketing tools

### For Buyers

Buyers enjoy:
- A wide variety of products from multiple sellers
- Competitive pricing and special deals
- Secure payment options
- Order tracking and history
- Customer support and dispute resolution

### Technology & Performance

Deployed on Microsoft Azure's infrastructure, the marketplace offers:
- Fast page load times for excellent user experience
- High availability and reliability
- Scalable architecture to handle growing traffic
- Mobile-responsive design for shopping on any device
- Secure data handling and privacy protection

Whether you're an entrepreneur looking to sell products online or a shopper seeking great deals, Marketplace Application provides a complete e-commerce solution.
